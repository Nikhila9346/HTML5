Let's have a quick recap of Indian Cinema.

The Cinema of India, consisting of motion pictures made by the Indian film industry, has had a large effect on world cinema since the second half of the 20th century.Indian cinema is made up of various film industries, including Hindi cinema, which makes motion pictures in the Hindi language and is one of the biggest film industries in the country. In 2021, Telugu cinema became the largest film industry in India in terms of box office receipts. Major centers of film production across the country include Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Kochi, Bangalore, Bhubaneswar-Cuttack, and Guwahati.
